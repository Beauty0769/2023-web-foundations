L'Homme

L'Homme represents the man and distingusing characteristics of the various perfumes of Le Pew d'Cote d'ivoire.

Chocolat

Chocolat perfume has dark and mysterious elements. On a cool day the notes and inviting. On a warm day the scent mingles with the warmth of one's skin and can either allure one forward or may have to take a step back. On a hot day the notes are fierce and can be deemed and presumptious.

La Amour

La Amour is lovely and kind. The notes suggests a certain level of passivity. Allowing one to come in and meander around. Wondering from notes of warmth to cool tones of dark hues.

Boudin Jaune

Sunny and bright, Boudin Jaune, one feels a sense of accomplishment and achievement. Always waiting and wanting bringing all notes together that would not be seen as complementary.

Trahir

The parfum imbodies the scene of two raging bulls, one of the last century and the other from this time sphere battling for relevant placement, using the users biology to mix it's own intrepretation of odour for the parfum.

Sugar

So sweet and nice, calm fragrance of vanilia yet rough and jarring notes of cinnamon. Expecting tranquility, yet getting an opposing scent of pistacio slamming against the mounds of nutmeg infused papaya, rocking a watermelon filtered limeade. Ice cold on the skin. It's profundity dimmed by the cataclymic fusion of spices.
